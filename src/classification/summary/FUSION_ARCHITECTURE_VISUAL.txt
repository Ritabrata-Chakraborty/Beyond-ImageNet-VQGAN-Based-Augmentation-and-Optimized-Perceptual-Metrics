╔═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║                        ATTENTION CONSISTENCY LOSS                           ║
╠═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╣
║                                                                             ║
║  Uses the 7 Attention Maps from Transformer Branch:                         ║
║                                                                             ║
║  📍 1. Fault-Specific Loss (λ=1.0)                                          ║
║      ├─ Create frequency mask for fault-relevant bands                      ║
║      ├─ Measure attention in these regions                                  ║
║      └─ Encourage focus on lower frequency bands                            ║
║                                                                             ║
║  ⏱️ 2. Temporal Consistency Loss (λ=0.5)                                    ║
║      ├─ Calculate attention differences along time axis                     ║
║      ├─ Penalize large jumps                                                ║
║      └─ Ensure smooth attention over time                                   ║
║                                                                             ║
║  🔗 3. Cross-Scale Alignment Loss (λ=0.3)                                   ║
║      ├─ Compare attention across 7 blocks                                   ║
║      ├─ Minimize L2 distance between consecutive layers                     ║
║      └─ Ensure consistent focus through network depth                       ║
║                                                                             ║
║  Total Attention Loss = 1.0×L_fault + 0.5×L_temp + 0.3×L_scale              ║
║                                                                             ║
║  Training Loss = CrossEntropy + attention_weight × Attention_Loss           ║
║  (attention_weight gradually increases from 0 to 0.5)                       ║
║                                                                             ║
